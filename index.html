Our Story

Tradition meets innovation

A Culinary Journey

Founded in 2005, New Ram Sai has been serving authentic Indian cuisine to food enthusiasts for over 15 years. What started as a small family restaurant has now grown into multiple branches across the city, each maintaining the same quality and tradition.

Our chefs bring decades of experience from different regions of India, ensuring that every dish is prepared with authentic spices and traditional cooking methods. We take pride in using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients while staying true to the rich culinary heritage of India.
